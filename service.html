Our Sevices

Pedicure

A pedicure is a professional foot care treatment designed to cleanse, smooth, and refresh the feet.

Poly Gel Nails

Poly Gel nails are a lightweight, hybrid nail enhancement that combines the strength of acrylic with the flexibility of gel. This system allows for smooth application, easy shaping, and a natural-looking finish.

Lashes

Eyelash extensions are semi-permanent fibers carefully applied to each natural lash to enhance length, volume, and definition. This treatment creates a fuller, more polished look without the need for mascara.

Piercing

Piercing is a professional beauty service where a sterile needle is used to create a small opening in the skin for wearing jewelry.
