Services For Every Occations

Russian Manicure

A Russian manicure is a precise nail technique that focuses on cleaning and refining the cuticle area using an electric drill. It creates a neat, clean look and allows polish or gel to sit perfectly under the cuticle, making nails look longer and helping the manicure last longer..

Acrylic Nails

Acrylic nails are created by combining a liquid monomer and powder to form a strong, durable extension on the natural nails. They’re perfect for adding length, strength, and any shape you want. Acrylics are long-lasting and can be styled with colors, designs, or charms to match any look.

Baib Nails

BIAB nails use a builder gel applied from a bottle to strengthen and grow the natural nails. It’s perfect for clients who want healthier, stronger nails without extensions. BIAB gives a smooth, natural look, lasts long, and helps prevent breakage while encouraging natural nail growth..

Pedicure

A pedicure is a foot care treatment that cleans, shapes, and refreshes the toenails and feet. It includes soaking, scrubbing, cuticle care, and nail trimming to keep the feet smooth and healthy. Finished with polish or gel, a pedicure leaves the feet looking neat, soft, and well-groomed.
